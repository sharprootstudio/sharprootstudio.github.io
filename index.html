About the Studio

Where Craft
Meets Vision

Hi, I'm Siddhant — the designer behind Sharp Root Studio. Based in the heart of Maharashtra near Kolhapur, I'm a self-taught graphic designer with a passion for creating visuals that actually work.

From scroll-stopping social media posts to full brand identities and AI-powered marketing visuals, I bring a sharp eye and creative precision to every project. My work lives at the intersection of traditional design craft and cutting-edge AI tools.

I also bring a unique perspective as an Ayurveda (BAMS) student — making me especially suited for wellness, herbal, and health brand design.
